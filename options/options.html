About DyslexiaAssist

DyslexiaAssist is designed to help people with dyslexia and other reading difficulties by providing customizable accessibility features.

Features:

  • Dyslexia-friendly fonts (including OpenDyslexic)
  • Adjustable text spacing and sizing
  • Reading guides and visual aids
  • Color filters and contrast adjustments
  • Animation reduction
  • Link highlighting

Tips for Better Reading:

  • Use the OpenDyslexic font for improved letter recognition
  • Increase line spacing to 1.5 or higher
  • Try different color overlays to find what works best
  • Use the reading guide when reading long passages
  • Adjust brightness based on your environment
